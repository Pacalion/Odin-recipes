Akume Recipes

Akume is a popular togolese dish appreciated by peaple. It is made from ground maize and fermented cassava. It is often paired with ademe fetri or tomato sauce.

Igredients

  • 200g of ground maize
  • 100g of fermented cassava
  • 1L of water
  • Mixe the ground maize and the fermented cassava in 0,5L of water in a saucepan./li>
  • Place the saucepan on a stove.
  • Gradualy stir with a spoon until the mixture become firm and slightly grey.
